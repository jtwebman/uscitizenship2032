# Design System: USCitizenship2032.com

## Design Philosophy
Authoritative, trustworthy, non-partisan, modern, and American without being flag-wavy. Think "constitutional reform institution" not "political campaign."

## Why These Colors
- **Teal + Gold** reads as "institutional reform" — not any political party
- **No red, no blue** as dominant colors — completely sidesteps partisan coding
- **Gold** evokes the Constitution, official seals, American heritage
- **Deep teal** carries the trust of blue + the renewal of green, while being politically unclaimed
- **Warm neutrals** prevent the palette from feeling cold or technocratic
- **Amber CTAs** are proven to drive action without being aggressive

## Color Palette

### Primary
| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|------|-----|-------|
| Primary | Deep Teal | `#0A5E6C` | Headers, links, secondary buttons |
| Secondary | Constitutional Gold | `#C49A2A` | Accents, underlines, highlights |
| Dark base | Rich Charcoal | `#1E2328` | Nav, hero backgrounds, footer |
| Light base | Warm White | `#F7F5F0` | Page backgrounds |

### Supporting
| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|------|-----|-------|
| CTA/Action | Warm Amber | `#D4790E` | Primary buttons, urgent CTAs |
| Success | Muted Green | `#3A7D5C` | Progress bars, state map fills |
| Text primary | Near Black | `#2A2D32` | Body copy |
| Text secondary | Warm Gray | `#6B7280` | Supporting copy, captions |
| Border/divider | Light Gray | `#E2DFD9` | Subtle separators |

## Application Rules
- **Nav/header**: Charcoal `#1E2328` bg, Warm White text, Gold accent
- **Hero sections**: Charcoal or Teal bg, large white text, gold underlines
- **Primary CTA buttons**: Amber `#D4790E` with white text
- **Secondary buttons**: Teal `#0A5E6C` with white text
- **Body content**: Warm White `#F7F5F0` bg, Near Black `#2A2D32` text
- **Progress/maps**: Green `#3A7D5C` for completed, Light Gray `#E2DFD9` for pending
- **Blockquotes/callouts**: Gold left border, light teal background tint

## Typography
- **Headings**: Bold sans-serif (Inter or similar) — modern authority
- **Body**: Clean sans-serif — readable, professional
- **Whitespace-heavy layouts** — signals confidence and clarity

## Design Patterns (from successful reform org research)
- Dark hero sections with large typography for gravitas
- Strong CTAs above the fold
- Social proof counters (supporters, states)
- State progress maps as central visual
- Infographic-style "how it works" explainers
- Testimonial/endorsement sections
- Minimal color palette: 1 primary + 1 accent + neutrals
